Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how PT Signal Delapan Belas ("we", "our", "us") collects and uses information when you use Jurnapod services (Backoffice, POS, and API).

Information We Collect

  • Account information: email, role, and access permissions.
  • Business data: items, prices, invoices, payments, and journal entries.
  • POS transaction data and audit logs for operational traceability.
  • Technical data: device information, IP address, and timestamps for security.

How We Use Information

  • Authenticate users and authorize access.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Jurnapod services.
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • Maintain audit trails for compliance and security.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with trusted service providers (such as hosting and infrastructure vendors) to operate the service. If you use Google SSO, Google provides authentication information to us. We only use it to verify your identity.

Cookies and Sessions

We use HTTP-only cookies for session refresh tokens when enabled. These cookies are used for authentication and security and are not used for advertising.

Data Retention

We retain data as long as needed to provide services and comply with legal and operational requirements. Audit logs may be retained longer for compliance and security.

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data against unauthorized access or disclosure. No system is completely secure, so please use strong passwords and keep credentials confidential.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data, subject to legal and contractual requirements.
